Welcome

We are a research group in the Center for Agricultural Data Analytics (CADA) at the University of Arkansas Division of Agriculture, led by Dr. Samuel B. Fernandes. Our work sits at the intersection of statistics, genetics, and machine learning, with the goal of making plant breeding faster and more predictable across diverse environments.
